Intro

I'm a final-year student from the National University of Singapore (NUS) majoring in Data Science & Analytics (Hons.) with a 2nd major in Computer Science (focus area in Artificial Intelligence).

My project span a wide range of topics. Spanning from image classification (computer vision), text recommender systems (NLP), fall detection models (anomaly detection) and more, my diverse array of machine learning models covers a broad spectrum of industries and problem domains. Check out the work I've done!

Fall Detection Device

This was a project under one of my Co-Curricular Activities (CCA) from NUS called NUS MedTech. The project is named "Project Morphine" and involves building a wearable fall detection device to detect falls in elderly persons.

As you might have guessed, this project's interdisciplinary nature brought together students from the biomedical engineering major, computer science major, and data science major (that's me!) together. Under the data science team which consists of two members, I was directly involved in the entire data science lifecycle for this project - from data collection, data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, model building, model evaluation, and deployment. Our initial prototype garnered the Gold award from the Biomedical Engineering Society (BES) design competition. Click to find out more!

Skills: Anomaly Detection, Variational Autoencoders, Autoencoders, Tensorflow, Git, Docker, Google Firebase

University Recommender System

As prospective university students in Singapore finish their A levels or diploma, it can be extremely confusing and difficult to decide which university to enrol in. Some local universities do not publish module information publicly online, which further adds to the dilemma. This project aims to help students interested in data science to choose which local university they should consider based on their career interests. These recommendations also take into account the skills demanded by the job market today using an automated web scraper for JobStreet and MyCareersFuture.

From scraping job description information from job posting sites, to prompt engineering for generating data using ChatGPT, all the way to leveraging Named Entity Recognition (NER) and Word2Vec models and more, this project displays a wide spectrum of tools & techniques used throughout different stages of the data science lifecycle. Click to find out more!

Skills: Web Scraping (Selenium), ChatGPT, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Named Entity Recognition (NER) models, Word Vectorizer models (Word2Vec, TFIDF), SpaCy, NLTK, Gensim, Unit Testing, Git, Docker

Weapon Detection

With gun-related crimes on the rise, this project aims to develop a weapon detection system to classify images into three categories: Normal (no weapon), Carrying (weapon detected, not posing a threat to anyone), Threat (weapon detected and is a threat).

This project taught me the basics of computer vision. It displays knowledge of image augmentation techniques, Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), as well as the Grad-CAM method for model interpretability. Click to find out more!

Skills: Image Classification, Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), Image Preprocessing, Image Augmentation, OpenCV, PIL, Tensorflow, Grad-CAM, Data Cleaning
